Human Soup

About the recipe

Human soup is a delicacy from Mars! Back when the Mars government were sick of humans sending probes and rovers to Mars, so they decided to launch an attack against Earth. They ended up capturing 5 humans and we're thinking what to do with them.

The great Mars chef, Mars Chef, decided to experiment with humans as food, and he came up with this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • 100 pound of live humans
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil a large cauldron of water, and let the humans simmer in it for 3 days
  2. Season with salt, pepper and basil to taste
  3. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving

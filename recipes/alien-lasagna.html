Alien Lasagna


About the recipe

Lasagna is a mix of meat, cheese, sauce and pasta, and this one isn't all that different, except it has that alien taste to it ;)

The Italians have fooled you all into thinking that they were the ones who invented the lasagna, but actually it was the aliens! Jupiter residents, to be more precise. Alien lasagna is the exact same dish you'd expect human lasagna to be, and thats exactly what we're making today! So get your ovens ready and let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausage
  • 3/4 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon alien seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until brown
  2. Strin in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, alien seasoning, salt, pepper and parsely
  4. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally
  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  6. Cook noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes
  7. Drain noodles, rinse with cold water
  8. in a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley and salt
  9. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  10. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish, arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over sauce
  11. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture, and top with a third of the mozzarella cheese slices
  12. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meet sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan
  13. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  14. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes
